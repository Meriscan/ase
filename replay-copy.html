Replays

Because the world needed another website like this!

This website was originally meant as a repository for people's replays, both video and inp files. It is meant to evoke the style of websites that hosted inp files around 2005 for various player. Players can submit their replays, regardless if they are associated with ASE.

You can submit your average superplay here. Submission are accepted when;
- It's the first replay for a certain game.
- It's the first replay for a category within a certain game. (Within reason)
- The replay is higher scoring than the current replay, or has a notable gimmick (Giga Wing without using Reflect).
There can be exceptions in very specific cases.
