What? ASE

The show, the team and whatever terrible idea we get next

At some point in March (sounds about right), Plasmo found an old crypt full of inp's that old players shared to show their replays. Believe it or not, people actually used these things. Pearl has since tried to get as many working as possible. Together, they started a show called Average Super Players Enjoy, showing all the replays they got working. The show can be found on Pearl's Twitch channel around Wednesday 2am EST / 6am GMT. Maybe you'll even be awake while one of the episodes is airing.

Besides the show, ASE has also become a circlejerk team.

ASE Membership

You can just ask for an invite for ASE on Discord. All that matters is that we know you're chill and fit well into the group. That's all we're trying to be in the end.
